Top Attractions

South Beach

Miami Beach is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning sandy beaches, lively nightlife, and iconic Art Deco architecture. Whether you're seeking relaxation under the sun or exciting cultural experiences, Miami Beach offers the perfect blend of beauty and energy.

Virginia Key Beach Park

Virginia Key Beach Park is a historic coastal gem just minutes from downtown Miami, offering scenic views, tranquil waters, and a glimpse into the city’s rich cultural past. With biking trails, kayak rentals, and family-friendly picnic spots, it's a perfect escape for both relaxation and recreation.

Wynwood

Wynwood is Miami’s vibrant arts district, famous for its colorful street murals, trendy galleries, and creative energy. Home to the iconic Wynwood Walls, this neighborhood buzzes with art, music, and a lively mix of shops, breweries, and eateries.

Vizcaya Museum & Gardens

A breathtaking historic estate blending European elegance with tropical beauty, Vizcaya features ornate architecture, lush gardens, and waterfront views—ideal for a peaceful, scenic escape.

Brickell City Centre

A sleek, open-air shopping and dining complex in the heart of Miami’s financial district, Brickell City Centre offers luxury brands, rooftop bars, and upscale eateries.

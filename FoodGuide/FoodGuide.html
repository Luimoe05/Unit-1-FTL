Food Guide

Joe's Stone Crab

11 Washington Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33139

A Miami institution since 1913, Joe’s is famous for its Florida stone crab claws served with signature mustard sauce. The menu also includes beloved classics like fried chicken and key lime pie. Great for those who love seafood.

L’Atelier de Joël Robuchon

151 NE 41st St, Miami, FL 33137

Located in the Miami Design District, this Michelin-starred French restaurant offers refined cuisine with modern flair in an elegant, open-kitchen setting. Ideal for those looking for a luxurious dinner.

Boia De

5205 NE 2nd Ave, Miami, FL 33137

A small, hidden gem in a strip mall, Boia De offers creative Italian-inspired cuisine and has earned a Michelin star for dishes like beef tartare and pastas with bold flavors. Amazing for those seeking a good and unique Italian cuisine experience

Sanguich De Miami

2057 SW 8th St, Miami, FL 33135

This Cuban sandwich shop in Little Havana serves up perfected classics like the Cubano and Pan con Bistec in a bright, stylish space. Great for those looking to get a taste of one of Cuba's most famous dishes.
